ABOUT BLIND ESPORTS

Blind eSports is a South Asia based Esports organization that was formed in April 2019. They have rosters playing for both, Multiplayer and Battle Royale in Call of Duty: Mobile Esports. BLIND also competes for Esports titles like Clash of Clans , Free Fire and PUBG Mobile. The current active rosters for Blind eSports are acquired from LiTx Revenants and Team APEX.
